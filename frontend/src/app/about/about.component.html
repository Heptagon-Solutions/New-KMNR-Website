About KMNR

KMNR is a free-format college radio station broadcasting at 89.7 MHz in Rolla, MO. KMNR's license is held by the University of Missouri's board of curators. KMNR is run by its executive board who are responsible for maintaining the station and ensuring compliance with FCC rules. KMNR Executive board are elected at the end of the Spring Semester and serve for a year. KMNR's faculty advisor is {{ advisor }}. KMNR's Public File is accessible at the station.

Contact

KMNR is connected to social media, and down with the youths. For any inquiries, including website related inquires, please feel free to contact the station.

E-Board

KMNR's E-Board are the heart and soul behind the station. They are tasked with enforcing KMNR's constitution and complying with FCC regulations. The E-board is selected annually from the active DJs by a general election and serve a one year term. Any specific inquires about the station or their position can be directed appropriately.

Hiring

KMNR is always looks for new DJs to volunteer at the station at the beginning of every semester. If you meet the following criteria, then you're eligible to become a DJ:

  • Must be a Missouri S&T student.
  • Must not currently be on academic probation.
  • Should have at least two semesters left on campus.

We also prefer that you've completed at least one semester here at Missouri S&T to be sure that you're accustomed to college life, but freshmen and transfer students are always considered and accepted! All applicants are interviewed and a group of trainees are selected out of those people. These trainees then are trained on the various aspects of KMNR. Once a trainee completes all the requirements, they then become a fully-licensed DJ.

If you're interested, applications can be picked up at the station in the first floor of Altman Hall during the first few weeks of every semester. Be sure to not miss out!

In addition to bringing in new DJs, KMNR also hires librarians to take care of our extensive music library. If you are eligible for work study and wish to become a librarian, email us at stationmanager@kmnr.org.

If you have any questions about becoming a DJ, email us at personnel@kmnr.org or give us a call at (573) 341-4272.

Roadshow

Need some life added to your party? Need some sweet tunes for your next event? Then look no further than the KMNR Roadshow!

KMNR Roadshow gives you the chance to have a part of the KMNR library at your next event. A part of KMNR, the KMNR Roadshow is run by Missouri S&T students. We can provide the music, equipment, and labor to keep your event rockin'. We have one of the largest non-commercial musical libraries in the country to give you the strange, rare, or even run of the mill musical playlist that you desire.

If you're interested, you can contact the roadshow director by sending an email to roadshow@kmnr.org.
